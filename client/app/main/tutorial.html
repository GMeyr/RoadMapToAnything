Welcome to our tutorial! Let's get started on how to create a roadmap:

Sign into your account and select "Create a roadmap" from the menu:



Fill out the title and description for each step (which represents one resource):



Each node has an information box that has an editable title, description, and link(s). Click to immediately edit the information.



To immediately create a new node to add to the roadmap, simply click the "+"! Nodes can be quickly added this way and your roadmap can easily be exapanded from here.
